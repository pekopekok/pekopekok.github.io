Basic Cooking Supplies For College Students A Guide

Written by Jordan Jul 26, 2023 · 3 min read

As a college student, cooking can be both fun and daunting. You may not have access to a fully-equipped kitchen, and you might be limited by budget and time constraints. However, with some basic cooking supplies, you can still whip up delicious and nutritious meals without breaking the bank. In this guide, we’ll be discussing the essential cooking supplies for college students.

Pots and Pans

Pots and pans are essential for cooking anything from pasta to stir-fry. As a college student, you don’t need a whole set of expensive cookware. Instead, invest in a few basic pieces such as a small saucepan, a medium-sized pot, and a skillet. Look for durable and affordable options, such as non-stick pans, that are easy to clean.

Utensils

Utensils are also important for cooking, and you don’t need a lot of them. A basic set of utensils includes a spatula, a wooden spoon, a slotted spoon, and tongs. These tools are versatile and can be used for a variety of dishes.

Cutting Board

A cutting board is necessary for chopping vegetables, fruits, and meats. Opt for a small, lightweight board that’s easy to store and clean. You can choose between plastic and wooden cutting boards, but keep in mind that plastic ones are easier to maintain.

Chef Knife

A good chef knife is a game-changer when it comes to cooking. It makes chopping and slicing a breeze and can be used for a variety of tasks. Look for a knife with a comfortable grip and a sharp blade. You don’t need an expensive knife, but investing in a decent one will make cooking much easier.

Measuring Cups and Spoons

Measuring cups and spoons are essential for baking and cooking. They ensure that you use the right amount of ingredients and help you achieve accurate results. Look for a set that includes the standard measurements of cups and spoons.

Mixing Bowls

Mixing bowls are useful for preparing and serving food. You can use them to mix ingredients, toss salads, or store leftovers. Look for a set of bowls that are lightweight, stackable, and easy to clean.

Blender

A blender is not a must-have, but it can be handy for making smoothies, purees, and soups. If you have a small budget, you can opt for a handheld immersion blender that’s portable and easy to use.

Slow Cooker

A slow cooker is a great investment for college students who want to cook healthy and hearty meals without too much effort. You can use it to make stews, chili, and soups. Slow cookers are also great for meal prep as you can make large batches of food that can be stored and reheated throughout the week.

Storage Containers

Storage containers are crucial for storing leftovers and meal prep. Look for containers that are microwave and dishwasher safe and have tight-fitting lids to prevent spills. You can opt for reusable containers or disposable ones, depending on your preference.

Cleaning Supplies

Lastly, cleaning supplies are essential for maintaining a clean and hygienic kitchen. You’ll need dish soap, a sponge, and a dish towel to clean your cooking supplies after use. You can also invest in a small bottle of all-purpose cleaner to wipe down counters and surfaces.

Conclusion

With these basic cooking supplies, you’ll be able to cook delicious and healthy meals without spending too much money or time. Remember to shop smart and look for affordable and durable options that fit your needs. Happy cooking!
