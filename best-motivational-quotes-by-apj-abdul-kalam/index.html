Welcome to our blog post about the best motivational quotes by APJ Abdul Kalam. APJ Abdul Kalam was a renowned Indian scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was also known as the "Missile Man of India" for his contributions to the development of ballistic missile technology.

Quote 1

"You have to dream before your dreams can come true."

This quote by APJ Abdul Kalam emphasizes the importance of dreaming and having a vision for your life. Without a dream, you cannot achieve anything. You need to have a clear vision of what you want to achieve in life and work towards it.

Quote 2

"Don't take rest after your first victory because if you fail in second, more lips are waiting to say that your first victory was just luck."

This quote by APJ Abdul Kalam reminds us that success is not a one-time achievement. You need to keep working hard and stay focused to maintain your success. Failure is a part of the journey, and you need to be prepared to face it.

Quote 3

"All of us do not have equal talent. But, all of us have an equal opportunity to develop our talents."

This quote by APJ Abdul Kalam emphasizes the importance of hard work and dedication. Even if you do not have the natural talent for something, you can still achieve it through hard work and practice. Everyone has the opportunity to develop their talents and reach their full potential.

Quote 4

"If you want to shine like a sun, first burn like a sun."

This quote by APJ Abdul Kalam emphasizes the importance of hard work and sacrifice. If you want to achieve greatness, you need to be willing to put in the effort and make sacrifices. You need to work hard and persevere through the challenges to achieve your goals.

Quote 5

"Thinking should become your capital asset, no matter whatever ups and downs you come across in your life."

This quote by APJ Abdul Kalam emphasizes the importance of having a positive mindset. Your thoughts and beliefs shape your reality, and you need to cultivate a positive mindset to overcome the challenges in life. Your thoughts should be your greatest asset, and you should use them to achieve your goals.

Quote 6

"Be more dedicated to making solid achievements than in running after swift but synthetic happiness."

This quote by APJ Abdul Kalam emphasizes the importance of focusing on long-term goals rather than short-term happiness. True happiness comes from achieving your goals and making a meaningful impact on the world. You need to be dedicated to your goals and work hard to achieve them.

Quote 7

"You cannot change your future, but you can change your habits, and surely your habits will change your future."

This quote by APJ Abdul Kalam emphasizes the importance of habits and how they shape your future. Your habits determine your success, and you need to cultivate good habits to achieve your goals. You have the power to change your habits and shape your future.

Quote 8

"Learning gives creativity, creativity leads to thinking, thinking provides knowledge, and knowledge makes you great."

This quote by APJ Abdul Kalam emphasizes the importance of learning and how it leads to greatness. Learning is a continuous process, and it helps you develop your creativity and critical thinking skills. With knowledge, you can achieve anything and make a positive impact on the world.

Quote 9

"Success is when your signature becomes an autograph."

This quote by APJ Abdul Kalam emphasizes the importance of leaving a legacy. Success is not just about achieving your goals; it's about making a meaningful impact on the world. When your signature becomes an autograph, it means that you have made a lasting impact on the world and left a legacy.

Quote 10

"You see, God helps only people who work hard. That principle is very clear."

This quote by APJ Abdul Kalam emphasizes the importance of hard work and how it leads to success. God helps those who help themselves, and you need to work hard to achieve your goals. With hard work and dedication, you can achieve anything you set your mind to.

Conclusion

These are some of the best motivational quotes by APJ Abdul Kalam. His words inspire us to work hard, dream big, and achieve greatness. We hope these quotes have inspired you to pursue your dreams and make a positive impact on the world.
