What Does "When Will You Wake Up" Mean in Hindi?

"When will you wake up" translates to "जब आप उठेंगे" (jab aap utheinge) in Hindi. This phrase is often used to ask someone about their morning routine or when they plan to start their day.

How to Use "When Will You Wake Up" in Hindi

If you're traveling to India or speaking with Hindi-speaking individuals, you can use "when will you wake up" to ask about their daily schedule. Here are some examples:

  • "आज सुबह आप कब उठेंगे?" (Aaj subah aap kab utheinge?) - When will you wake up this morning?
  • "आप रोज सुबह कितने बजे उठते हैं?" (Aap roz subah kitne baje uthte hain?) - What time do you usually wake up in the morning?

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Say "When Will You Wake Up" in Hindi

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to say "when will you wake up" in Hindi:

  1. Start with the phrase "जब आप" (jab aap), which means "when you."
  2. Add the verb "उठेंगे" (utheinge), which means "will wake up."
  3. Put the time or day after the verb to complete the sentence.

For example, "जब आप उठेंगे" (jab aap utheinge) can be followed by "कल सुबह" (kal subah) to mean "when will you wake up tomorrow morning?"

Tips for Learning Hindi Phrases

Learning a new language can be challenging, but with the right approach, you can make progress quickly. Here are some tips for learning Hindi phrases:

  • Watch Hindi movies or TV shows with subtitles to improve your listening skills.
  • Read Hindi books or articles to expand your vocabulary.
