As Labor Day approaches, residents of Kansas City are looking for fun and exciting activities to celebrate the long weekend. This article will provide you with some great ideas for things to do on Labor Day in Kansas City. Whether you want to enjoy the outdoors or relax indoors, there is something for everyone to enjoy.

Table of Contents

  • Outdoor Activities
  • Indoor Activities
  • Food and Drink
  • Music and Entertainment

Outdoor Activities

Kansas City is known for its beautiful parks and outdoor activities, so why not take advantage of the long weekend and enjoy some time outdoors?

Visit the Kansas City Zoo

The Kansas City Zoo is the perfect place to spend a day with family and friends. With over 1,700 animals and 200 acres of beautiful grounds, there is something for everyone to enjoy. Take a walk through the African Plains exhibit or see the penguins in the Helzberg Penguin Plaza. Don't forget to stop by the Australian Outback exhibit to see the kangaroos and wallabies.

Take a Hike

Kansas City has many beautiful hiking trails that are perfect for a Labor Day weekend adventure. The Lakeside Nature Center Trail is a 3.3-mile loop that takes you through a beautiful forest and along a scenic lake. The Blue River Parkway Trail is another great option, with over 17 miles of paved paths that wind through parks, neighborhoods, and scenic areas.

Indoor Activities

If you prefer to beat the heat and stay indoors, there are plenty of activities to keep you entertained.

Visit the National World War I Museum

The National World War I Museum is a must-see attraction in Kansas City. The museum features exhibits and artifacts that tell the story of the Great War, including interactive displays and multimedia presentations. Don't miss the opportunity to see the Liberty Memorial Tower, which offers stunning views of the city.

Go to a Movie

Take advantage of the long weekend and catch a new release at one of Kansas City's many movie theaters. AMC Barrywoods 24 and Alamo Drafthouse Mainstreet are two popular options that offer comfortable seating, great food, and excellent sound systems.

Food and Drink

What better way to celebrate Labor Day than by enjoying some delicious food and drink?

Attend the Kansas City Renaissance Festival

The Kansas City Renaissance Festival is a fun and unique way to spend Labor Day weekend. The festival features live entertainment, jousting tournaments, and plenty of food and drink options. Don't forget to try the giant turkey legs!

Visit a Brewery

Kansas City is home to many great breweries, so why not take a tour and enjoy some delicious beer? Boulevard Brewing Company and KC Bier Co. are two popular options that offer tours and tastings.

Music and Entertainment

Kansas City has a thriving music and entertainment scene, so why not take advantage of some of the great events happening over Labor Day weekend?

Attend the Kansas City Irish Fest

The Kansas City Irish Fest is a three-day event that celebrates Irish culture with live music, dance performances, and plenty of food and drink options. Don't miss the chance to see traditional Irish bands like The Elders and The High Kings.

Go to a Concert

Many popular musicians and bands stop in Kansas City on their tours, so be sure to check out the schedule and see if any of your favorites are playing over Labor Day weekend. The Sprint Center and Starlight Theatre are two popular venues that host concerts and other events.

Conclusion

With so many great activities and events happening in Kansas City over Labor Day weekend, there is no reason to be bored. Whether you want to enjoy the outdoors, relax indoors, or explore the city's food and entertainment scene, there is something for everyone to enjoy.
