What is Republic Day in India?

Republic Day is a national holiday in India celebrated on 26th January every year to honour the date on which the Constitution of India came into effect in 1950. It marks the day when India became a republic, a sovereign democratic nation with its own constitution and elected leaders. The day is celebrated with great enthusiasm and pride across the country with parades, flag hoisting, cultural programs, and patriotic songs.
