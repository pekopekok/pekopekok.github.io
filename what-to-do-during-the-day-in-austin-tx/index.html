If you're planning a trip to Austin, TX, you might be wondering what to do during the day. Austin is a vibrant city with plenty of activities to keep you busy, but if you're looking for a more relaxed experience, there are plenty of options as well. In this guide, we'll cover some of the best things to do in Austin during the day that will help you unwind and enjoy the city at a more leisurely pace.

1. Visit Zilker Park

Zilker Park is a 350-acre park located in the heart of Austin that offers a variety of relaxing activities. You can take a stroll through the botanical garden or take a dip in the spring-fed pool. If you're feeling more adventurous, you can rent a kayak or paddleboard and explore Lady Bird Lake. There are also plenty of picnic areas where you can enjoy a relaxing lunch.

2. Explore the Lady Bird Lake Trail

The Lady Bird Lake Trail is a 10-mile trail that circles Lady Bird Lake and provides a scenic route for runners, walkers, and cyclists. The trail is shaded by large oak trees and offers beautiful views of the downtown skyline. Along the trail, you'll find plenty of spots to stop and relax, including benches, picnic tables, and even a few swings.

3. Take a Dip at Barton Springs Pool

Barton Springs Pool is a natural spring-fed pool located in Zilker Park. The pool is three acres and is perfect for swimming or just relaxing on a hot day. The water temperature stays around 68 degrees year-round, making it a refreshing escape from the Texas heat.

4. Visit the Blanton Museum of Art

The Blanton Museum of Art is located on the University of Texas at Austin campus and features over 18,000 works of art from around the world. The museum has a diverse collection that includes modern and contemporary art, European paintings, and Latin American art. The museum is a great place to spend a relaxing afternoon exploring the galleries.

5. Shop at the Austin Farmers Market

The Austin Farmers Market is a great place to relax and enjoy the local culture. The market is open every weekend and features local vendors selling fresh produce, artisanal cheeses, and handmade crafts. You can wander through the stalls, sample some of the local treats, and enjoy the live music.

6. Relax at Lake Travis

Lake Travis is a popular spot for locals and tourists alike. The lake is perfect for swimming, boating, and fishing. If you're looking for a more relaxed experience, you can rent a pontoon boat and spend the day lounging on the water. There are also plenty of restaurants and bars located around the lake where you can enjoy a cold drink and some fresh seafood.

7. Take a Yoga Class

Austin is known for its vibrant yoga community, and there are plenty of studios located throughout the city. Taking a yoga class is a great way to relax and unwind while also getting a good workout. Many studios offer classes for all levels, so even if you're a beginner, you can still enjoy the experience.

8. Visit the Austin Nature and Science Center

The Austin Nature and Science Center is located in the Zilker Park area and features exhibits on local wildlife, geology, and ecology. There are also hiking trails and a nature playground where kids can explore and learn about the natural world.

9. Explore the Texas State Capitol

The Texas State Capitol is a beautiful building located in downtown Austin. The building is open to the public and offers guided tours that provide a fascinating look into Texas history and government. The grounds surrounding the Capitol are also worth exploring, with plenty of statues and monuments to admire.

10. Enjoy Live Music at the Continental Club

Austin is known as the "Live Music Capital of the World," and no visit to the city would be complete without experiencing some of its famous music scene. The Continental Club is a historic venue that has hosted some of the biggest names in music over the years. You can enjoy a cold beer and listen to live music in a relaxed and intimate setting.

Tips for a Relaxing Day in Austin

  • Dress comfortably and wear sunscreen if you plan on spending time outdoors.
  • Bring a water bottle to stay hydrated.
  • Check the weather forecast before heading out to make sure you're prepared for the conditions.
  • Take your time and enjoy the experience - Austin is a laid-back city, so there's no need to rush.
  • Be sure to try some of the local food and drinks - Austin is known for its delicious cuisine and craft beer scene.

Conclusion

There are plenty of relaxing things to do in Austin during the day, whether you're looking to explore the outdoors, visit a museum, or simply unwind with some live music. By following our guide and tips, you can have a memorable and enjoyable experience in this vibrant and welcoming city.
