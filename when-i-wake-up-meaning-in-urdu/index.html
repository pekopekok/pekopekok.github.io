What is the Meaning of "When I Wake Up" in Urdu?

The phrase "when I wake up" is commonly used to refer to the moment when someone opens their eyes after sleeping. In Urdu, the meaning of "when I wake up" can be translated to "جب میں جاگوں".

How to Use "When I Wake Up" in Urdu?

If you want to use the phrase "when I wake up" in Urdu, you can use it in the following ways:

  • جب میں جاگوں، میں نے پہلے چائے پینا ہے۔ (When I wake up, I have to drink tea first.)
  • جب میں جاگوں، میں اپنی نماز پڑھوں گا۔ (When I wake up, I will pray my prayers.)
  • جب میں جاگوں، میں اپنے دوست کو کال کر دوں گا۔ (When I wake up, I will call my friend.)
