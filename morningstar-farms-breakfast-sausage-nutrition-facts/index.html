How to Cook MorningStar Farms Breakfast Sausage

Here's how to cook MorningStar Farms Breakfast Sausage:

  1. Preheat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the MorningStar Farms Breakfast Sausage patties to the skillet.
  3. Cook for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until heated through and golden brown.
  4. Remove from heat and serve.
