Things To Do On Christmas Day In Dubai

Are you looking for some fun activities to do on Christmas Day in Dubai? Look no further! Dubai is a city filled with holiday cheer, and there are plenty of things to do to celebrate this festive season. Whether you're looking for family-friendly activities or something more adventurous, we have you covered. Here are some of the best things to do on Christmas Day in Dubai.

Visit the Dubai Miracle Garden

If you're a fan of flowers and Christmas lights, then the Dubai Miracle Garden is the perfect place to visit on Christmas Day. This garden boasts over 45 million flowers, and during the holiday season, it is adorned with stunning Christmas decorations and lights. You can take a stroll through the garden and enjoy the sights and smells of the flowers, or you can take a tour and learn more about the garden's history and design.

Go Ice Skating

If you're looking for a more active way to celebrate Christmas Day, then ice skating is a great option. Dubai has several ice rinks, including the Dubai Ice Rink at the Dubai Mall and the Olympic-sized ice rink at the Dubai Mall of the Emirates. You can rent skates and glide around the rink while enjoying the holiday decorations and music.

Enjoy a Christmas Brunch

Many restaurants in Dubai offer special Christmas Day brunches, where you can enjoy a delicious meal with family and friends. Some popular options include the Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah, the Ritz-Carlton Dubai, and the Atlantis, The Palm. Reservations are recommended as these brunches can get quite busy.

Shop at the Christmas Markets

If you're looking for unique Christmas gifts, then head to one of Dubai's Christmas markets. These markets offer a range of handmade gifts, decorations, and food. Some popular markets include the Dubai Winter Festival and the Christmas Market at Habtoor Palace. These markets are also a great place to soak up the holiday atmosphere and enjoy some festive entertainment.

Take a Desert Safari

If you're looking for something more adventurous, then a desert safari is a great option. You can take a tour of the Dubai Desert Conservation Reserve, ride a camel, go dune bashing, and enjoy a traditional Arabian dinner under the stars. Many tour companies offer special Christmas Day packages, so be sure to check them out.

Watch a Christmas Movie

If you're looking for a more low-key activity, then why not watch a Christmas movie? Dubai has several cinemas, including the Vox Cinemas at the Dubai Mall and the Reel Cinemas at Dubai Marina Mall. You can catch a screening of a classic Christmas movie or watch a new release.

Attend a Christmas Concert

Many venues in Dubai host Christmas concerts, featuring local and international performers. Some popular venues include the Dubai Opera and the Coca-Cola Arena. You can enjoy some festive music and get into the holiday spirit.

Take a Christmas Cruise

If you're looking for a unique way to celebrate Christmas Day, then why not take a Christmas cruise? You can enjoy a festive meal while cruising around Dubai's waterways and taking in the stunning views. Many tour companies offer Christmas Day cruises, so be sure to book in advance.

Visit Ski Dubai

If you're missing the snow and cold weather, then Ski Dubai is the perfect place to visit on Christmas Day. This indoor ski resort offers a range of activities, including skiing, snowboarding, and tobogganing. You can also enjoy a hot chocolate or a meal at one of the resort's restaurants.

Relax at the Beach

If you're looking for a more laid-back way to celebrate Christmas Day, then head to one of Dubai's beaches. You can soak up the sun, enjoy a swim, and take in the beautiful views of the Arabian Gulf. Some popular beaches include Jumeirah Beach and Kite Beach.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are plenty of things to do on Christmas Day in Dubai. Whether you're looking for family-friendly activities or something more adventurous, Dubai has something for everyone. So, gather your loved ones and celebrate the holiday season in this beautiful and vibrant city.
