Does Apple Watch Show How Many Calories You Burn In A Day

Written by Alvine Apr 03, 2023 · 3 min read

If you’re trying to keep track of your fitness goals, you may be wondering if your Apple Watch can tell you how many calories you burn in a day. The answer is yes! The Apple Watch has a built-in feature that tracks your daily calorie burn, among other health metrics. In this article, we’ll explore how the Apple Watch calculates your daily calorie burn and how you can use this information to achieve your fitness goals.

Description

The Apple Watch uses a variety of sensors to track your daily activity, including your heart rate, movement, and GPS location. By analyzing this data, the watch can estimate how many calories you burn throughout the day. This information is displayed in the Activity app on your iPhone, where you can view your daily calorie burn as well as other health metrics like steps taken and distance traveled.

How to

To view your daily calorie burn on your Apple Watch, simply open the Activity app and swipe left to view the Calories screen. Here, you’ll see a graph that shows your calorie burn throughout the day, as well as your current progress towards your daily calorie goal. You can also view your calorie burn for previous days by swiping right on the Calories screen.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions for viewing your daily calorie burn on your Apple Watch:

  1. Open the Activity app on your Apple Watch
  2. Swipe left to view the Calories screen
  3. View your daily calorie burn graph and progress towards your goal
  4. Swipe right to view your calorie burn for previous days

Tips

If you’re trying to lose weight or maintain a healthy weight, tracking your daily calorie burn can be a helpful tool. Here are some tips for using this information to achieve your fitness goals:

  • Set a daily calorie goal that is challenging but achievable
  • Use the Activity app to track your progress throughout the day
  • Make adjustments to your diet and exercise routine based on your daily calorie burn
  • Stay motivated by celebrating your achievements and setting new goals

Solution

The Apple Watch is a powerful tool for tracking your daily activity and achieving your fitness goals. By using the built-in calorie tracking feature, you can get a better understanding of how many calories you burn throughout the day and use this information to make smarter choices about your diet and exercise routine. Whether you’re trying to lose weight, maintain a healthy weight, or simply live a more active lifestyle, the Apple Watch can help you get there.

FAQ

Q: How does the Apple Watch calculate my daily calorie burn?

A: The Apple Watch uses a combination of sensors, including your heart rate, movement, and GPS location, to estimate how many calories you burn throughout the day.

Q: Can I adjust my daily calorie goal on my Apple Watch?

A: Yes, you can adjust your daily calorie goal in the Activity app on your iPhone. Simply go to the Calories screen, tap on the “…” button, and choose “Change Move Goal” to adjust your daily goal.

Q: Can I track my calorie burn for specific workouts on my Apple Watch?

A: Yes, the Apple Watch has a variety of workout tracking features that allow you to track your calorie burn for specific activities like running, cycling, and swimming.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • The Apple Watch provides accurate and detailed information about your daily calorie burn
  • You can use this information to make smarter choices about your diet and exercise routine
  • The Activity app makes it easy to track your progress and set new goals

Cons

  • The Apple Watch can be expensive compared to other fitness trackers
  • Some users may find the calorie tracking feature to be too basic
  • The accuracy of the calorie tracking feature may be affected by factors like age, weight, and fitness level
