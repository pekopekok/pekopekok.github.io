If you're traveling solo to Las Vegas and wondering what to do during the day, you're in luck. Las Vegas is a city that never sleeps, and there are plenty of things to keep you entertained during the day. In this post, we will explore some of the best things to do in Vegas alone during the day.

1. Visit the High Roller Observation Wheel

The High Roller Observation Wheel is one of the most popular attractions in Las Vegas. It's a giant Ferris wheel that offers breathtaking views of the city. The ride takes about 30 minutes and costs around $30 per person. You can choose to ride during the day or at night, but we recommend riding during the day to get the best views.

2. Explore the Strip

The Las Vegas Strip is home to some of the most famous casinos, hotels, and attractions in the world. You can spend hours walking around and exploring everything the Strip has to offer. Some must-see attractions include the Bellagio Fountains, the Eiffel Tower at Paris Las Vegas, and the Volcano at The Mirage.

3. Relax at the Pool

Las Vegas is known for its pools, and there are plenty of options to choose from. Whether you're looking for a party atmosphere or a more relaxed vibe, there's a pool for you. Some of the best pools in Vegas include the Mandalay Bay Beach, the Venetian Pool Deck, and the Wynn Pool.

4. Take a Tour of the Hoover Dam

The Hoover Dam is a marvel of engineering and one of the most impressive sights in the United States. You can take a tour of the dam and learn about its history and construction. The tour takes about two hours and costs around $30 per person.

5. Visit the Neon Museum

The Neon Museum is a unique museum that showcases the history of Las Vegas through its iconic neon signs. You can take a guided tour of the museum and see signs from some of the most famous casinos and hotels in Vegas. The tour takes about one hour and costs around $20 per person.

6. Shop at the Forum Shops at Caesars Palace

The Forum Shops at Caesars Palace is one of the most luxurious shopping destinations in Vegas. You can find everything from designer clothing to high-end jewelry to unique souvenirs. Even if you're not looking to buy anything, it's worth a visit just to see the stunning architecture and decor.

7. Visit the Bellagio Gallery of Fine Art

The Bellagio Gallery of Fine Art is a must-visit for art lovers. The gallery features rotating exhibits of some of the world's most famous artists, including Picasso, Monet, and Warhol. The admission fee is around $20 per person, but it's well worth it for the chance to see some incredible works of art.

8. Go on a Food Tour

Vegas is known for its incredible food scene, and going on a food tour is a great way to experience it. You can choose from a variety of tours that showcase different types of cuisine, from Italian to Mexican to Asian. Some popular food tour companies in Vegas include Lip Smacking Foodie Tours and Vegas Valley Food Tours.

9. Attend a Show

Las Vegas is home to some of the best shows in the world, from Cirque du Soleil to magic shows to concerts by famous musicians. You can find a show to suit any taste or budget. Some of the most popular shows in Vegas include O by Cirque du Soleil, Blue Man Group, and Celine Dion's residency at The Colosseum at Caesars Palace.

10. Take a Day Trip to Red Rock Canyon

If you're looking to escape the hustle and bustle of the city, a day trip to Red Rock Canyon is the perfect solution. The canyon is just a short drive from the Strip and offers stunning views of the desert landscape. You can go hiking, rock climbing, or just enjoy a picnic in the great outdoors.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are plenty of things to do in Vegas alone during the day. Whether you're looking for adventure, relaxation, or culture, there's something for everyone in this vibrant city. So go ahead and explore all that Vegas has to offer!
