Welcome to our guide on what are good scary movies for 11 year olds. Halloween is around the corner and it’s time to watch some spooky movies with your kids. But, finding age-appropriate scary movies can be a challenge. In this guide, we'll provide you with a list of good scary movies for 11 year olds. So, let's get started!

How to Choose Age-Appropriate Scary Movies for 11 Year Olds?

Before we dive into the list of good scary movies for 11 year olds, let's talk about how to choose age-appropriate scary movies. Here are a few things to consider:

1. Rating

Check the rating of the movie before watching it with your kids. Look for movies that are rated PG or PG-13. These movies usually have mild scares and are appropriate for kids aged 11 and above.

2. Theme

Make sure the movie has a theme that your child can handle. Some kids are scared of ghosts, while others may be scared of monsters or zombies. Choose a movie that has a theme that your child can handle.

3. Gore

Avoid movies that have too much gore, violence, or blood. These movies are not appropriate for kids and can cause nightmares.

List of Good Scary Movies for 11 Year Olds

Now that you know how to choose age-appropriate scary movies, here is a list of good scary movies for 11 year olds:

1. The Corpse Bride (2005)

The Corpse Bride is a stop-motion animated movie that tells the story of a young man who accidentally marries a corpse. It’s a spooky and funny movie that is appropriate for kids aged 11 and above.

2. Coraline (2009)

Coraline is a stop-motion animated movie that tells the story of a young girl who discovers a secret door in her new home. It’s a spooky and adventurous movie that is appropriate for kids aged 11 and above.

3. Goosebumps (2015)

Goosebumps is a live-action movie based on the popular book series by R.L. Stine. It’s a spooky and fun movie that is appropriate for kids aged 11 and above.

4. The House with a Clock in Its Walls (2018)

The House with a Clock in Its Walls is a live-action movie that tells the story of a young boy who discovers a magical house. It’s a spooky and adventurous movie that is appropriate for kids aged 11 and above.

5. The Nightmare Before Christmas (1993)

The Nightmare Before Christmas is a stop-motion animated movie that tells the story of Jack Skellington, the king of Halloween Town, who discovers Christmas Town. It’s a spooky and fun movie that is appropriate for kids aged 11 and above.

Tips for Watching Scary Movies with Kids

Here are a few tips for watching scary movies with kids:

1. Watch with Your Kids

Watch the movie with your kids so that you can answer any questions they may have and reassure them if they get scared.

2. Turn on the Lights

Turn on the lights while watching the movie so that your kids feel safe and secure.

3. Take Breaks

Take breaks during the movie to give your kids a chance to calm down if they get scared.

FAQs

Q. Are horror movies appropriate for 11 year olds?

A. No, horror movies are not appropriate for 11 year olds as they usually have too much gore, violence, or blood.

Q. Can scary movies cause nightmares?

A. Yes, scary movies can cause nightmares if they are too scary or if your child is easily scared.

Pros and Cons of Watching Scary Movies with Kids

Pros:

- It can be a fun and bonding experience for parents and kids.

- It can help kids face their fears in a safe environment.

Cons:

- It can cause nightmares and anxiety in some kids.

- It can be too scary for some kids.

That’s it for our guide on what are good scary movies for 11 year olds. We hope you found it helpful. Remember to choose age-appropriate scary movies and watch with your kids for a fun and safe Halloween experience!
