Introduction

If you are a bread lover, you must have tried a bread roll with butter and cheese at least once in your life. This delicious snack is perfect for breakfast, lunch, or as a mid-day snack. But if you are watching your calorie intake, you might be wondering how many calories in a bread roll with butter and cheese. In this article, we will answer this question and provide you with some tips on how to make the perfect bread roll with butter and cheese.

How Many Calories in a Bread Roll with Butter and Cheese?

A bread roll with butter and cheese is a calorie-dense snack that can quickly add up if you are not careful. The number of calories in a bread roll with butter and cheese depends on the type of bread, butter, and cheese you use. Let's take a look at each component:

Bread Roll

The number of calories in a bread roll depends on the size and type of bread. On average, a small bread roll (about 40g) has around 120-150 calories. However, if you use a larger roll, the calorie count can go up to 200-250 calories.

Butter

Butter is a calorie-dense ingredient that can quickly increase the calorie count of your bread roll. One tablespoon of butter (about 14g) has around 100-120 calories. If you use more than one tablespoon of butter, the calorie count will increase accordingly.

Cheese

Cheese is another ingredient that can significantly increase the calorie count of your bread roll. The number of calories in cheese depends on the type and amount of cheese you use. On average, one slice of cheese (about 28g) has around 100-120 calories. If you use more than one slice of cheese or a higher calorie cheese, the calorie count will increase accordingly.

Based on the above information, a bread roll with butter and cheese (using a small bread roll, one tablespoon of butter, and one slice of cheese) has around 320-380 calories.

How to Make a Delicious Bread Roll with Butter and Cheese?

Ingredients

  • 1 small bread roll
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 slice of cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C.
  2. Cut the bread roll in half.
  3. Spread the butter on both halves of the bread roll.
  4. Place the cheese slice on one half of the bread roll.
  5. Place the other half of the bread roll on top of the cheese.
  6. Place the bread roll on a baking tray and bake in the oven for 5-7 minutes or until the cheese is melted and the bread roll is crispy.
  7. Remove from the oven and serve hot.

Tips for Making the Perfect Bread Roll with Butter and Cheese

  • Choose a high-quality bread roll for the best taste.
  • Use unsalted butter to control the amount of salt in your snack.
  • Choose a low-fat cheese to reduce the calorie count of your snack.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese for different flavors.
  • Toast the bread roll before adding the butter and cheese for a crispy texture.

Pros and Cons of Eating a Bread Roll with Butter and Cheese

Pros:

  • A bread roll with butter and cheese is a quick and easy snack that can satisfy your hunger.
  • It is a good source of carbohydrates and protein.
  • It can be customized with different types of bread, butter, and cheese for different flavors.

Cons:

  • A bread roll with butter and cheese is a calorie-dense snack that can contribute to weight gain if consumed in excess.
  • It is high in saturated fat and sodium, which can be detrimental to your health if consumed in excess.

FAQs

1. Is a bread roll with butter and cheese a healthy snack?

A bread roll with butter and cheese is not considered a healthy snack due to its high calorie, fat, and sodium content. However, it can be enjoyed in moderation as part of a balanced diet.

2. Can I use margarine instead of butter?

Yes, you can use margarine instead of butter. However, make sure to choose a margarine that is low in saturated fat and sodium.

3. Can I add other ingredients to my bread roll with butter and cheese?

Yes, you can add other ingredients such as ham, bacon, tomato, or lettuce to your bread roll with butter and cheese. However, keep in mind that the calorie count will increase accordingly.

Conclusion

A bread roll with butter and cheese is a tasty snack that can be enjoyed in moderation as part of a balanced diet. However, it is important to be mindful of the calorie, fat, and sodium content and to choose high-quality ingredients for the best taste. We hope this article has answered your question on how many calories in a bread roll with butter and cheese and provided you with some tips on how to make the perfect snack.
