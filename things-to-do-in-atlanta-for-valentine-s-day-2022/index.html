Things To Do In Atlanta For Valentine s Day 2022

Written by Bowie Apr 06, 2023 · 3 min read

.

Planning a special Valentine's Day 2022 in Atlanta? Look no further than this guide for things to do in the city of Atlanta. From romantic dinners to cozy picnics, there are plenty of options to make your Valentine's Day unforgettable. Here are some of the best things to do in Atlanta for Valentine's Day 2022.

1. Romantic Dinner

Start the evening off with a romantic dinner at one of Atlanta's finest restaurants. There are many options to choose from, including Italian, French, and American cuisine. Some of the top restaurants in Atlanta include Bacchanalia, Canoe, and Aria. Be sure to make reservations early to secure your table.

2. Cozy Picnic

Enjoy a cozy picnic with your loved one at one of Atlanta's parks. Piedmont Park, Grant Park, and Centennial Olympic Park are great options for a romantic picnic. Pack a basket with your favorite food and drinks and enjoy the beautiful scenery together.

3. Visit the Georgia Aquarium

The Georgia Aquarium is a great option for a Valentine's Day date. Explore the underwater world together and get up close with animals like whale sharks, dolphins, and sea otters. Book a special behind-the-scenes tour to make the experience even more memorable.

4. Take a Romantic Stroll at the Atlanta Botanical Garden

The Atlanta Botanical Garden is an ideal location for a romantic stroll. Enjoy the beautiful flowers and plants as you walk hand-in-hand with your loved one. The garden also offers special events throughout the year, including concerts and movie nights.

5. Visit the High Museum of Art

The High Museum of Art is a great option for art lovers. Explore the galleries together and admire the beautiful art on display. The museum also offers special events throughout the year, including lectures and workshops.

6. Take a Helicopter Tour

See Atlanta from above with a helicopter tour. This is a unique and exciting way to spend Valentine's Day with your loved one. Admire the beautiful city skyline as you soar through the air.

7. Catch a Movie at the Drive-In Theater

Experience a blast from the past with a visit to the drive-in theater. The Starlight Drive-In Theatre is a popular option in Atlanta. Pack some snacks and cuddle up in the car for a romantic movie night.

8. Visit the World of Coca-Cola

The World of Coca-Cola is a fun and interactive museum that explores the history of the Coca-Cola company. Taste different Coca-Cola products from around the world and learn about the iconic brand together.

9. Book a Couple's Spa Day

Relax and unwind with a couple's spa day. Atlanta has many options for luxurious spa experiences, including The Four Seasons, The Ritz-Carlton, and The Mandarin Oriental. Indulge in massages, facials, and other spa treatments together.

10. Attend a Wine Tasting

Enjoy a romantic evening of wine tasting at one of Atlanta's wineries. Château Élan Winery and Resort is a popular option and offers a variety of wine tasting experiences. Sip on delicious wines and enjoy each other's company. In conclusion, there are many things to do in Atlanta for Valentine's Day 2022. Whether you're looking for a romantic dinner, a cozy picnic, or an exciting adventure, Atlanta has something to offer every couple. Make this Valentine's Day unforgettable with one of these special experiences.
