What is 17 Day Green Tea Diet Meal Plan?

The 17 Day Green Tea Diet Meal Plan is a low-calorie diet that incorporates green tea into your daily routine. This diet is designed to help you lose weight, improve your digestion, and boost your metabolism. The diet is divided into four cycles, each lasting for 17 days, and each cycle has a different focus.

The first cycle is the Accelerate Cycle, which is designed to help you jumpstart your weight loss. During this cycle, you will eat a low-carbohydrate, high-protein diet, and you will drink green tea throughout the day to boost your metabolism.

The second cycle is the Activate Cycle, which is designed to help you continue losing weight while adding more healthy carbohydrates to your diet. During this cycle, you will eat more fruits and vegetables, and you will continue to drink green tea throughout the day.

The third cycle is the Achieve Cycle, which is designed to help you reach your weight loss goals. During this cycle, you will eat a balanced diet that includes lean protein, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates. You will also continue to drink green tea throughout the day to maintain your metabolism.

The fourth cycle is the Arrive Cycle, which is designed to help you maintain your weight loss and continue living a healthy lifestyle. During this cycle, you will eat a balanced diet, exercise regularly, and continue to drink green tea throughout the day.

How to Follow 17 Day Green Tea Diet Meal Plan?

To follow the 17 Day Green Tea Diet Meal Plan, you will need to make some changes to your eating habits and daily routine. Here are some steps to follow:

  1. Calculate your daily calorie needs and determine the number of calories you need to consume to lose weight.
  2. Choose foods that are high in protein, fiber, and healthy fats, and low in carbohydrates and sugar.
  3. Drink green tea throughout the day to boost your metabolism and stay hydrated.
  4. Exercise regularly, incorporating both cardio and strength training exercises into your routine.
  5. Stay motivated and track your progress by weighing yourself regularly and taking measurements.

Step-by-Step Guide to 17 Day Green Tea Diet Meal Plan

Accelerate Cycle (Days 1-17)

During the Accelerate Cycle, you will eat a low-carbohydrate, high-protein diet, and you will drink green tea throughout the day to boost your metabolism. Here is a sample meal plan:

  • Breakfast: Omelet with spinach, mushrooms, and feta cheese, and a cup of green tea
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with berries and almonds
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken breast with a side salad and a cup of green tea
  • Snack: Apple slices with almond butter
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with roasted asparagus and a cup of green tea

Activate Cycle (Days 18-34)

During the Activate Cycle, you will eat more fruits and vegetables, and you will continue to drink green tea throughout the day. Here is a sample meal plan:

  • Breakfast: Smoothie made with almond milk, banana, spinach, and chia seeds, and a cup of green tea
  • Snack: Carrots with hummus
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, tomatoes, and avocado, and a cup of green tea
  • Snack: Orange slices with pistachios
  • Dinner: Grilled shrimp with a side salad and a cup of green tea

Achieve Cycle (Days 35-51)

During the Achieve Cycle, you will eat a balanced diet that includes lean protein, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates. You will also continue to drink green tea throughout the day to maintain your metabolism. Here is a sample meal plan:

  • Breakfast: Whole grain toast with avocado and a poached egg, and a cup of green tea
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with granola and berries
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken sandwich with sweet potato fries and a cup of green tea
  • Snack: Pear slices with almond butter
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with quinoa and roasted vegetables, and a cup of green tea

Arrive Cycle (Days 52-68)

During the Arrive Cycle, you will eat a balanced diet, exercise regularly, and continue to drink green tea throughout the day. Here is a sample meal plan:

  • Breakfast: Overnight oats with almond milk, banana, and chia seeds, and a cup of green tea
  • Snack: Hard-boiled egg with celery sticks
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken wrap with mixed greens, cucumber, and hummus, and a cup of green tea
  • Snack: Blueberries with almonds
  • Dinner: Grilled steak with roasted vegetables and a cup of green tea

Tips for Success on 17 Day Green Tea Diet Meal Plan

Here are some tips to help you succeed on the 17 Day Green Tea Diet Meal Plan:

  • Drink plenty of water throughout the day to stay hydrated.
  • Choose lean protein sources, such as chicken breast, fish, and tofu.
  • Eat plenty of fruits and vegetables to get the vitamins and minerals your body needs.
  • Avoid processed foods, sugary drinks, and high-fat foods.
  • Exercise regularly, incorporating both cardio and strength training exercises into your routine.
  • Get plenty of rest and manage your stress levels.

FAQ about 17 Day Green Tea Diet Meal Plan

1. Is the 17 Day Green Tea Diet Meal Plan safe?

Yes, the 17 Day Green Tea Diet Meal Plan is safe for most people. However, if you have any health conditions or concerns, you should speak with your doctor before starting this diet.

2. How much weight can I expect to lose on the 17 Day Green Tea Diet Meal Plan?

The amount of weight you can expect to lose on the 17 Day Green Tea Diet Meal Plan depends on your starting weight, your calorie intake, and your exercise routine. However, most people can expect to lose 1-2 pounds per week on this diet.

3. Can I drink other beverages besides green tea on the 17 Day Green Tea Diet Meal Plan?

You can drink other beverages besides green tea, but you should avoid sugary drinks and high-calorie beverages. Stick to water, unsweetened tea, and black coffee if you need a caffeine boost.
