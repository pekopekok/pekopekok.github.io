Exercises

One of the best ways to get bigger shoulders without weights is by doing bodyweight exercises. Here are a few exercises that you can do:

Pushups

Pushups are a great way to work your shoulders, chest, and triceps. To do a pushup, start in a plank position with your hands shoulder-width apart. Lower your body until your chest touches the ground, then push back up to the starting position.

Pike Pushups

Pike pushups are a variation of pushups that focus more on your shoulders. Start in a downward dog position, with your hands and feet on the ground and your hips in the air. Lower your head towards the ground, then push back up to the starting position.

Handstand Pushups

Handstand pushups are a more advanced exercise that require a lot of upper body strength. Start in a handstand against a wall, then lower your head towards the ground and push back up to the starting position.

Diet

Diet is also an important factor in getting bigger shoulders. Here are a few tips:

Eat Enough Protein

Protein is essential for building muscle, so make sure you're getting enough in your diet. Good sources of protein include chicken, fish, eggs, and beans.

Eat Enough Calories

In order to build muscle, you need to be in a calorie surplus. Make sure you're eating enough calories to support muscle growth.

Stay Hydrated

Drinking enough water is important for muscle growth and recovery. Aim to drink at least 8 glasses of water a day.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips for getting bigger shoulders:

Be Consistent

Consistency is key when it comes to building muscle. Make sure you're exercising regularly and sticking to your diet.

Get Enough Rest

Rest is important for muscle recovery, so make sure you're taking rest days and getting enough sleep.

Challenge Yourself

In order to see progress, you need to challenge yourself. Try increasing the number of reps or sets you do, or try a more advanced variation of an exercise.

Solution

By incorporating bodyweight exercises and a healthy diet into your routine, you can get bigger shoulders without weights. Remember to be consistent, get enough rest, and challenge yourself in order to see progress.

FAQ

Can I get bigger shoulders without weights?

Yes, you can get bigger shoulders without weights by doing bodyweight exercises and eating a healthy diet.

What are the best bodyweight exercises for shoulders?

Some of the best bodyweight exercises for shoulders include pushups, pike pushups, and handstand pushups.

How long does it take to see results?

Results will vary depending on your starting point and how consistent you are with your workouts and diet. However, with consistent effort, you should start to see results within a few weeks.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Can be done anywhere
  • No need for expensive equipment
  • Can improve overall upper body strength

Cons:

  • May not be as effective as weight training
  • May require more time and effort to see results
  • May be difficult to progress to more advanced exercises
