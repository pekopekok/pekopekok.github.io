Description

Ab workouts are an essential part of any fitness routine, and they don't have to be complicated or require a gym membership. With these simple easy ab workouts home, you can work on your core strength and get those abs in shape. Whether you are a beginner or have been working out for a while, these exercises can help you achieve your fitness goals.

How to

The best part about these simple easy ab workouts home is that you don't need any equipment. All you need is a comfortable mat or carpeted floor. You can do these exercises anywhere, anytime. These exercises are perfect for those who want to work on their abs but don't have a lot of time.

Step by Step

Here are some simple easy ab workouts home that you can do:

1. Plank

Start in a push-up position with your arms straight and your hands shoulder-width apart. Keep your body straight and hold this position for as long as you can. Repeat for 3 sets of 30 seconds each.

2. Bicycle Crunches

Lie on your back with your knees bent and your hands behind your head. Lift your shoulder blades off the ground and bring your left elbow to your right knee. Then, bring your right elbow to your left knee. Repeat for 3 sets of 15 reps each.

3. Russian Twist

Sit on the floor with your knees bent and your feet flat on the ground. Lean back slightly and lift your feet off the ground. Twist your torso to the left and touch the floor with your hands. Then, twist to the right and touch the floor with your hands. Repeat for 3 sets of 15 reps each.

Tips

Here are some tips to get the most out of your simple easy ab workouts home:

  • Engage your core muscles while doing these exercises.
  • Breathe in and out slowly and deeply.
  • Start slow if you are a beginner and gradually increase the intensity.
  • Don't overdo it. Give your muscles time to rest and recover.
