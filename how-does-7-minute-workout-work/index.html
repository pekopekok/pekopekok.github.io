Are you looking for a quick and efficient way to stay fit and healthy? Look no further than the 7 minute workout! This workout has gained popularity in recent years due to its convenience and effectiveness. In this article, we will discuss how the 7 minute workout works and how you can incorporate it into your daily routine.

How to Do 7 Minute Workout

The 7 minute workout is a high-intensity circuit training workout that consists of 12 exercises performed for 30 seconds each with 10 seconds of rest in between. The exercises target various muscle groups and can be done anywhere without any equipment. Here’s how to do the 7 minute workout:

1. Jumping Jacks

Start by doing jumping jacks for 30 seconds. This exercise works your cardiovascular system and gets your blood pumping.

2. Wall Sit

Next, do a wall sit for 30 seconds. This exercise works your lower body and helps to build strength in your legs.

3. Push-ups

After the wall sit, do push-ups for 30 seconds. This exercise works your chest, shoulders, and triceps.

4. Abdominal Crunches

Next, do abdominal crunches for 30 seconds. This exercise targets your core and helps to strengthen your abdominal muscles.

5. Step-ups onto a Chair

After abdominal crunches, do step-ups onto a chair for 30 seconds. This exercise works your lower body and helps to build strength in your legs.

6. Squats

Next, do squats for 30 seconds. This exercise works your lower body and helps to build strength in your legs and glutes.

7. Triceps Dips on a Chair

After squats, do triceps dips on a chair for 30 seconds. This exercise works your triceps and helps to tone your arms.

8. Plank

Next, hold a plank for 30 seconds. This exercise targets your core and helps to strengthen your abdominal muscles.

9. High Knees Running in Place

After the plank, do high knees running in place for 30 seconds. This exercise works your cardiovascular system and gets your heart rate up.

10. Lunges

Next, do lunges for 30 seconds. This exercise works your lower body and helps to build strength in your legs.

11. Push-ups with Rotation

After lunges, do push-ups with rotation for 30 seconds. This exercise works your chest, shoulders, triceps, and core.

12. Side Plank

Finally, hold a side plank for 30 seconds on each side. This exercise targets your core and helps to strengthen your abdominal muscles.

Step by Step Guide to 7 Minute Workout

Here’s a step by step guide to help you get started with the 7 minute workout:

Step 1: Warm-up

Before you start the 7 minute workout, it’s important to warm up your body. You can warm up by doing some light cardio exercises such as jogging or jumping jacks for a few minutes.

Step 2: Set a Timer

Once you’re warmed up, set a timer for 7 minutes. You can use a timer on your phone or any other device.

Step 3: Perform the Exercises

Start performing the exercises in the order listed above. Remember to do each exercise for 30 seconds with 10 seconds of rest in between.

Step 4: Cool Down

Once you’ve completed the 7 minute workout, cool down by doing some light stretching exercises. This will help to prevent injury and reduce muscle soreness.

Tips for a Successful 7 Minute Workout

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your 7 minute workout:

  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water before, during, and after your workout.
  • Wear comfortable workout clothes and shoes that provide adequate support.
  • Focus on proper form and technique for each exercise to prevent injury.
  • Start with a slower pace and gradually increase your intensity as you get stronger.
  • Don’t be afraid to modify the exercises to fit your fitness level and ability.

7 Minute Workout as a Solution to a Busy Lifestyle

The 7 minute workout is a great solution for people who have busy schedules and don’t have a lot of time to spend on exercise. It can be done anywhere, without any equipment, and only takes 7 minutes to complete. This makes it easy to fit into any daily routine, whether you’re at home, at work, or on the go.

Frequently Asked Questions About 7 Minute Workout

1. Is the 7 minute workout effective?

Yes, the 7 minute workout is effective for improving cardiovascular fitness, building strength, and burning calories.

2. Can I do the 7 minute workout every day?

Yes, you can do the 7 minute workout every day, but it’s important to give your body time to rest and recover between workouts.

3. Can I modify the exercises to fit my fitness level?

Yes, you can modify the exercises to fit your fitness level and ability. For example, you can do push-ups on your knees instead of your toes if you’re a beginner.

Pros and Cons of 7 Minute Workout

Pros:

  • Convenient and time-efficient
  • No equipment required
  • Can be done anywhere
  • Effective for improving fitness and health

Cons:

  • May not be suitable for people with certain medical conditions
  • May not be challenging enough for advanced exercisers
  • May not provide enough variety for some people

Overall, the 7 minute workout is a great option for people who want to stay fit and healthy but don’t have a lot of time to spend on exercise. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can start incorporating the 7 minute workout into your daily routine and enjoy the many benefits it has to offer!
