Description

The total body strength training gym routine by Joanna Soh is designed to help people build muscle and increase their overall fitness levels. This workout plan includes a variety of exercises that target different muscle groups in the body, including the chest, back, arms, shoulders, legs, and abs. The routine is designed to be done at a moderate to high intensity level, which means that it will challenge your muscles and help you see results quickly.

How to

If you're interested in trying out the total body strength training gym routine by Joanna Soh, here's how to get started:

  1. First, make sure that you have access to a gym with a variety of equipment, including free weights, resistance bands, and machines.
  2. Next, warm up your body with some light cardio, such as jogging on a treadmill or using a stationary bike.
  3. Start with the first exercise in the routine, and do 10-12 reps with a weight that challenges you but allows you to maintain proper form.
  4. Rest for 30-60 seconds, then move on to the next exercise in the routine.
  5. Repeat this process for all of the exercises in the routine, taking breaks as needed.
  6. After completing the routine, cool down with some stretching exercises to prevent injury and promote recovery.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step breakdown of the total body strength training gym routine by Joanna Soh:

Chest Exercises

  • Bench Press
  • Incline Bench Press
  • Chest Flyes

Back Exercises

  • Lat Pulldowns
  • Seated Rows
  • Deadlifts

Arm Exercises

  • Bicep Curls
  • Tricep Pushdowns
  • Hammer Curls

Shoulder Exercises

  • Seated Shoulder Press
  • Upright Rows
  • Lateral Raises

Leg Exercises

  • Squats
  • Lunges
  • Leg Press

Ab Exercises

  • Plank
  • Crunches
  • Leg Raises

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of the total body strength training gym routine by Joanna Soh:

  • Start with a weight that challenges you but allows you to maintain proper form.
  • Gradually increase the weight as you get stronger.
  • Take breaks as needed, but try to keep the rest periods between exercises short to keep your heart rate up.
  • Stay hydrated throughout the workout.
  • Use proper form to prevent injury and get the most out of each exercise.

Solution

The total body strength training gym routine by Joanna Soh is an effective solution for people who want to build muscle and increase their overall fitness levels. This workout plan includes a variety of exercises that target different muscle groups in the body, making it a well-rounded and comprehensive routine.

FAQ

What equipment do I need to do this routine?

You'll need access to a gym with a variety of equipment, including free weights, resistance bands, and machines.

How long does the routine take?

The length of the routine will depend on how many sets and reps you do, but it typically takes between 45 minutes to an hour to complete.

How often should I do this routine?

You can do this routine 2-3 times per week, with at least one day of rest in between each workout.
