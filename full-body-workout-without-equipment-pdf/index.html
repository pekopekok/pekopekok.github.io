Step 2: Read the Instructions

Step 3: Warm Up

It's important to warm up before you start the workout. You can do some light cardio exercises, such as jogging or jumping jacks, to get your heart rate up and prepare your muscles for the workout.

Step 4: Start the Workout

Once you're warmed up, you can start the workout. The PDF contains a series of exercises that you can perform in a circuit format. You'll do each exercise for a certain number of repetitions or for a set amount of time, depending on the instructions.

Tips for a Successful Full Body Workout without Equipment
