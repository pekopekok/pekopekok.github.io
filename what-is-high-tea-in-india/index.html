High tea is a meal that is typically served in the late afternoon or early evening and is a formal affair. In India, high tea has become a way for people to socialize and enjoy a relaxing afternoon. In this article, we will explore what high tea is in India, how to have the perfect high tea, and some tips and tricks to make your experience unforgettable.

What is High Tea in India?

High tea in India is a luxurious affair that involves the serving of tea, snacks, and light meals. It is usually served in the late afternoon or early evening and is a way for people to unwind and socialize. High tea in India is often associated with luxury hotels, where people can enjoy the experience in a grand setting. It is a popular activity among the elites, but it is not limited to them. Anyone can enjoy the experience of high tea in India.

How to Have the Perfect High Tea

Choose the Right Place

The first step to having the perfect high tea experience is to choose the right place. You want to pick a location that has a relaxed and elegant atmosphere. It should be a place where you can sit comfortably and enjoy your tea without feeling rushed. Luxury hotels are often a good choice for high tea as they offer a grand setting and excellent service.

Choose the Right Time

The timing of your high tea is also important. High tea is traditionally served between 3 pm and 5 pm. However, you can adjust the timing to suit your schedule. Make sure to book your high tea in advance, especially if you are going to a popular location.

Choose the Right Menu

The menu for high tea is usually a combination of sweet and savory items. It typically includes sandwiches, scones, cakes, and pastries. Make sure to choose a menu that suits your taste and preferences. If you have any dietary requirements, make sure to inform the restaurant in advance so that they can accommodate your needs.

Dress Appropriately

High tea is a formal affair, so it is important to dress appropriately. Men should wear a suit or jacket and trousers, while women should wear a dress or skirt and blouse. Avoid wearing jeans, shorts, or casual clothing.

Enjoy the Experience

The most important thing to remember when having a high tea experience is to enjoy it. Take your time and savor the food and the tea. Relax and socialize with your companions. High tea is a luxurious experience that should be savored and enjoyed.

Tips and Tricks for the Perfect High Tea Experience

Book in Advance

High tea is a popular activity, so it is important to book in advance. This will ensure that you get a table at your preferred location and time.

Try New Teas

High tea is all about the tea, so make sure to try new and different varieties. Ask the server for recommendations and try something new.

Don't Overindulge

High tea is a meal, but it is not a heavy one. Make sure to pace yourself and not overindulge in the food and tea.

Relax and Enjoy

High tea is a relaxing and enjoyable experience, so make sure to take your time and savor the moment. Don't rush through the experience, but instead, enjoy it to the fullest.

FAQ

What is the difference between high tea and afternoon tea?

High tea is a more substantial meal that is served in the late afternoon or early evening. Afternoon tea is a lighter meal that is served in the afternoon and typically includes sandwiches, scones, and pastries.

What is the dress code for high tea?

The dress code for high tea is typically formal. Men should wear a suit or jacket and trousers, while women should wear a dress or skirt and blouse.

What is the cost of high tea?

The cost of high tea varies depending on the location and menu. It can range from INR 1000 to INR 5000 per person.

Pros and Cons of High Tea

Pros

  • High tea is a luxurious and relaxing experience.
  • It is a great way to socialize and unwind.
  • The food and tea are delicious and of high quality.

Cons

  • High tea can be expensive.
  • It is a formal affair, so it may not be suitable for everyone.
  • The timing of high tea may not be convenient for everyone.

High tea is a luxurious and enjoyable experience that is perfect for anyone who wants to unwind and socialize. By following the tips and tricks outlined in this article, you can have the perfect high tea experience. So, book your high tea today and enjoy the experience!
