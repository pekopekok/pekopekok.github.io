Christmas is one of the most magical times of the year, especially in New York City, where the holiday spirit is felt all throughout the city. Whether you're a local or a visitor, there are plenty of things to do during Christmas in New York City. In this article, we'll share some of the best things to do during the holiday season in the Big Apple.

Visit Rockefeller Center

One of the most iconic Christmas traditions in New York City is visiting Rockefeller Center to see the famous Christmas tree. This massive tree stands at 75 feet tall and is adorned with thousands of lights and ornaments. While you're there, you can also go ice skating at the Rockefeller Center Ice Rink and visit the Top of the Rock observation deck for stunning views of the city.

Take a Stroll Through Central Park

Central Park is beautiful all year round, but it's especially magical during the holiday season. Take a stroll through the park and enjoy the festive decorations, including the holiday lights and the giant Christmas tree at the entrance of the park.

Go Shopping

New York City is home to some of the best shopping in the world, and the holiday season is the perfect time to take advantage of it. Whether you're looking for high-end designer stores or unique boutique shops, there's something for everyone in the city. Some of the best places to shop during the holiday season include Fifth Avenue, SoHo, and the Christmas markets throughout the city.

Check Out the Holiday Windows

One of the most popular holiday traditions in New York City is checking out the holiday windows at the department stores. Every year, stores like Macy's, Saks Fifth Avenue, and Bergdorf Goodman create elaborate holiday displays in their windows that are a must-see for anyone visiting the city during the holiday season.

Go Ice Skating

Ice skating is a quintessential winter activity, and New York City is home to some of the best ice skating rinks in the world. In addition to the ice rink at Rockefeller Center, you can also go ice skating at Central Park's Wollman Rink, Bryant Park's Winter Village, and the Brookfield Place rink in Battery Park City.

Catch a Holiday Show

New York City is known for its world-class theater scene, and the holiday season is no exception. Catch a performance of the Radio City Christmas Spectacular, the Nutcracker Ballet, or one of the many other holiday shows that are on offer throughout the city.

Explore the Holiday Markets

During the holiday season, New York City is home to a variety of holiday markets that offer unique gifts, food, and festive decorations. Some of the best markets include the Union Square Holiday Market, the Columbus Circle Holiday Market, and the Grand Central Holiday Fair.

See the Holiday Lights

The holiday lights in New York City are truly spectacular, and there are many different ways to see them. Take a walk through the city to see the lights on the buildings and streets, or take a tour of the holiday lights by bus or boat.

Enjoy Holiday Dining

New York City is home to some of the best restaurants in the world, and many of them offer special holiday menus during the season. Whether you're looking for a cozy spot to enjoy a festive meal or a high-end restaurant for a special occasion, there's something for everyone in the city.

Ring in the New Year

New Year's Eve in New York City is a once-in-a-lifetime experience. Join the crowds in Times Square to watch the ball drop or attend one of the many New Year's Eve parties that take place throughout the city.

Conclusion

There are so many things to do during Christmas in New York City, and this article only scratches the surface. Whether you're a local or a visitor, there's no shortage of holiday spirit in the Big Apple. From ice skating to holiday shows to shopping and dining, there's something for everyone to enjoy during the most wonderful time of the year.
