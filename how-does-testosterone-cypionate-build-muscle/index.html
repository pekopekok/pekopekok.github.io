What is Testosterone Cypionate?

Testosterone Cypionate is a synthetic version of the hormone testosterone. It is commonly used in the medical field to treat conditions such as hypogonadism, delayed puberty, and osteoporosis. It is also used by bodybuilders and athletes to build muscle mass and increase strength. Testosterone Cypionate is an injectable steroid and is available only by prescription.

How Does Testosterone Cypionate Build Muscle?

Testosterone Cypionate builds muscle by increasing protein synthesis in the body. Protein synthesis is the process by which cells build proteins. Testosterone Cypionate also increases nitrogen retention in the muscles. Nitrogen is an essential component of muscle protein. When nitrogen levels are high, the body is in an anabolic state, which means it is building muscle. Testosterone Cypionate also increases the production of red blood cells, which carry oxygen to the muscles. The more oxygen the muscles receive, the more they can work and grow.
