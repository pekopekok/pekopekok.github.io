Is 5 By 5 A Good Workout

Written by April Mar 19, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking for a workout routine that can help you build strength and muscle mass? If so, you might have heard about the 5 by 5 workout. This routine involves performing five sets of five reps of compound exercises such as squats, deadlifts, and bench presses. But is 5 by 5 a good workout? In this article, we’ll explore the pros and cons of this routine and provide some tips on how to make the most of it.

How to Do the 5 by 5 Workout?

Before we dive into the pros and cons of the 5 by 5 workout, let’s first discuss how to do it. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Choose Your Exercises

The 5 by 5 workout focuses on compound exercises that target multiple muscle groups at once. Some of the most popular exercises include:

  • Squats
  • Bench presses
  • Deadlifts
  • Overhead presses
  • Rows

Choose three to five of these exercises and plan to perform them in each workout.

Step 2: Determine Your Starting Weight

Before you start the 5 by 5 workout, you need to determine your starting weight for each exercise. This should be a weight that you can lift for five reps with good form but that feels challenging by the end of the set.

Step 3: Perform 5 Sets of 5 Reps

Once you have your starting weight, perform five sets of five reps for each exercise. Rest for one to two minutes between sets.

Step 4: Increase the Weight Each Workout

After each workout, increase the weight by 2.5 to 5 pounds for each exercise. This progressive overload will help you build strength and muscle mass over time.

Tips for Making the Most of the 5 by 5 Workout

Here are some tips on how to make the most of the 5 by 5 workout:

  • Focus on form: Since you’re using heavy weights, it’s important to focus on proper form to avoid injury.
  • Rest adequately: Take enough time to rest between sets to allow your muscles to recover.
  • Track your progress: Keep a workout journal to track your progress and increase the weight gradually.
  • Eat enough: To build muscle mass, you need to eat enough calories and protein. Make sure you’re fueling your body properly.

Pros and Cons of the 5 by 5 Workout

Like any workout routine, the 5 by 5 workout has its pros and cons. Here are some of them:

Pros:

  • Effective for building strength and muscle mass
  • Focuses on compound exercises that target multiple muscle groups
  • Easy to follow and progressive

Cons:

  • Requires access to heavy weights and equipment
  • Can be taxing on the central nervous system
  • May not be suitable for beginners or those with injuries

FAQ

Is the 5 by 5 workout good for beginners?

The 5 by 5 workout can be effective for beginners who have some experience with weightlifting and are comfortable with the exercises. However, it’s important to start with light weights and master proper form before increasing the weight.

Can women do the 5 by 5 workout?

Absolutely! The 5 by 5 workout is suitable for both men and women who want to build strength and muscle mass.

How long should I do the 5 by 5 workout?

You can do the 5 by 5 workout for as long as you see progress. Once you plateau, you can switch to a different workout routine or modify the 5 by 5 workout to suit your needs.

Conclusion

The 5 by 5 workout can be an effective way to build strength and muscle mass, especially if you’re comfortable with weightlifting and have access to the necessary equipment. Just remember to focus on form, rest adequately, track your progress, and fuel your body properly. With dedication and consistency, you can achieve your fitness goals with the 5 by 5 workout.
