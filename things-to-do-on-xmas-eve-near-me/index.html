Are you looking for things to do on Xmas Eve near your location? Look no further as we have compiled a list of fun activities to enjoy with friends and family. From outdoor activities to indoor games, there is something for everyone to make this Xmas Eve a memorable one. Let's dive into the list of things to do on Xmas Eve near me.

Indoor Activities

1. Have a Game Night

Bring out your favorite board games or card games and have a game night with your loved ones. Enjoy some snacks and drinks while playing games and having a good time.

2. Bake Cookies

Xmas Eve is the perfect time to bake some delicious cookies. Get creative with the icing and decorations and have fun with the whole family.

3. Watch a Movie

Snuggle up under a blanket and watch a Xmas movie with your family. There are many classic movies to choose from, such as Home Alone, Elf, and It's A Wonderful Life.

Outdoor Activities

1. Go Ice Skating

Many cities have outdoor ice skating rinks set up during the holiday season. Grab your skates and enjoy some time on the ice.

2. Decorate Your Neighborhood

Get together with your neighbors and decorate your street with Xmas lights and decorations. This is a great way to bring the community together and spread some holiday cheer.

3. Take a Walk to See the Lights

Take a stroll around the neighborhood and enjoy the beautiful Xmas lights and decorations. This is a great way to get some exercise and enjoy the festive season.

Other Activities

1. Volunteer at a Local Charity

Spread some holiday cheer by volunteering at a local charity. Many organizations need help during the holiday season, and this is a great way to give back to the community.

2. Attend a Xmas Eve Service

If you are religious, attending a Xmas Eve service is a great way to celebrate the holiday. Many churches have special services on Xmas Eve, so check your local listings.

3. Have a Xmas Eve Dinner

Invite your friends and family over for a Xmas Eve dinner. Cook up some traditional holiday dishes and enjoy the company of your loved ones.

Conclusion

These are just a few ideas for things to do on Xmas Eve near me. Whether you prefer indoor or outdoor activities, there is something for everyone to enjoy. Spend some quality time with your loved ones and make some great memories this holiday season.
