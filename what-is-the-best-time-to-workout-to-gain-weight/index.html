Are you trying to gain weight and build muscle? Working out is essential to achieving your fitness goals. However, the timing of your workouts can affect your weight gain journey. In this article, we will discuss the best time to workout to gain weight and how to optimize your workout routine for maximum results.

  • Afternoon Workouts
  • Evening Workouts

Early Morning Workouts

Many fitness enthusiasts prefer early morning workouts as it helps kickstart their day and gives them more energy throughout the day. Working out in the morning also helps improve your metabolism, which can help with weight gain. However, it is essential to fuel your body with the right nutrients before your workout.

Try having a small snack like a banana, protein shake, or oatmeal before your workout. This will help provide your body with the necessary energy to power through your workout without feeling too fatigued. Additionally, early morning workouts can help regulate your sleep cycle and improve the quality of your sleep.

Afternoon Workouts

Afternoon workouts can be beneficial for those who want to maximize their performance during their workout. Our body temperature is at its highest during the afternoon, which can help improve your overall performance during your workout. Additionally, afternoon workouts can help break up your workday and provide a much-needed energy boost.

However, it is essential to make sure you do not work out on a full stomach. Try to eat a small meal at least 2-3 hours before your workout to prevent any digestive issues during your workout.

Evening Workouts

Evening workouts can be beneficial for those who prefer to workout after work or have a busy schedule during the day. Working out in the evening can also help reduce stress levels and improve sleep quality. Additionally, evening workouts can help you unwind after a long day and improve your mood.

However, it is essential to make sure you do not work out too close to bedtime. Working out too close to bedtime can increase your heart rate and make it difficult to fall asleep. Try to finish your workout at least 2-3 hours before bedtime to allow your body to cool down and relax.

Tips for Optimizing Your Workout Routine

Regardless of the time you choose to work out, there are several tips you can follow to optimize your workout routine:

  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water before, during, and after your workout.
  • Warm-up before your workout to prevent any injuries.
  • Incorporate strength training exercises to build muscle and aid in weight gain.
  • Gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts to challenge your body.
  • Stretch after your workout to improve flexibility and prevent muscle soreness.

FAQ

Q: Can working out too much hinder weight gain?

A: Yes, working out too much without proper rest can hinder weight gain as it can lead to muscle fatigue and breakdown. It is essential to allow your muscles to rest and recover after each workout.

Q: Should I eat before or after my workout?

A: It is recommended to eat a small meal or snack before your workout to provide your body with the necessary energy. Additionally, it is essential to eat a post-workout meal to aid in muscle recovery and growth.

Q: How long should my workout be?

A: The duration of your workout depends on your fitness goals and fitness level. However, it is recommended to aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise or 15 minutes of high-intensity exercise.

In conclusion, the best time to workout to gain weight varies for each individual and depends on their schedule and preferences. However, it is essential to fuel your body with the right nutrients, stay hydrated, and incorporate strength training exercises to aid in weight gain. By following these tips and optimizing your workout routine, you can achieve your weight gain goals and improve your overall health and fitness.
