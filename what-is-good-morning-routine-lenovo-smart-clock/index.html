How to Set Up the Good Morning Routine

To set up the Good Morning Routine on your Lenovo Smart Clock, follow these steps:

Step 1: Open the Google Home App

The first step is to open the Google Home app on your smartphone or tablet. If you don't have the app yet, you can download it from the Google Play Store or the Apple App Store.

Step 2: Add Your Lenovo Smart Clock

The next step is to add your Lenovo Smart Clock to the Google Home app. To do this, tap the "+" icon in the top left corner of the screen, then tap "Set up device." Follow the on-screen instructions to add your Lenovo Smart Clock to the app.

Step 3: Customize Your Good Morning Routine

Once your Lenovo Smart Clock is added to the Google Home app, you can customize your Good Morning Routine. To do this, tap the "Settings" icon next to your Lenovo Smart Clock, then tap "Assistant." Scroll down to the "Routines" section and tap "Good Morning." From here, you can customize what happens when you say "Good Morning" to your Lenovo Smart Clock.
