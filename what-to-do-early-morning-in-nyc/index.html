Are you an early bird visiting the city that never sleeps? Fear not, for there are plenty of things to do early morning in NYC. Whether you're looking for a peaceful stroll or a quick workout, we've got you covered. Here's our guide to the best ways to start your day in New York City.

Central Park

Central Park is one of the best places to start your day in NYC. The park is open from 6am to 1am, so you can catch the sunrise or just take a morning stroll. The park is also home to many jogging paths, so you can get your heart rate up before the day begins. And if you're lucky, you may even spot some of the park's resident wildlife, such as squirrels, rabbits, and birds.

Brooklyn Bridge

If you're looking for a more active start to your day, consider walking across the Brooklyn Bridge. The bridge is open 24 hours a day, and offers stunning views of the city and the East River. The walk is about 1.3 miles long, so it's a great way to get some exercise while taking in the sights. And if you're feeling adventurous, you can even rent a bike and ride across the bridge.

The High Line

The High Line is a unique park built on an old elevated railway track. The park opens at 7am, and offers a peaceful walk with beautiful views of the city. The park is also home to many art installations and gardens, so there's plenty to see along the way. And if you're hungry, there are many food vendors along the High Line where you can grab a quick bite.

Morning Yoga

For a more peaceful start to your day, consider practicing yoga. Many yoga studios in NYC offer early morning classes, so you can start your day with some stretching and relaxation. Some studios even offer outdoor classes, so you can take in the morning air while practicing your downward dog.

Coffee and Breakfast

No morning is complete without a cup of coffee and breakfast. Luckily, NYC has no shortage of options. There are many coffee shops and cafes open early in the morning, so you can grab a cup of joe and a pastry before starting your day. And if you're looking for something more substantial, there are plenty of breakfast spots to choose from, ranging from classic diners to trendy brunch spots.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Experience the city in a peaceful and quiet atmosphere
  • Get some exercise or relaxation before the day begins
  • See the city from a different perspective
  • Find some of the best breakfast spots in the city

Cons:

  • Some activities may be weather-dependent
  • Not all businesses may be open early in the morning
  • Some activities may require a fee or reservation

FAQ

What time do most businesses open in NYC?

Most businesses in NYC open between 8am and 10am, but some may open earlier or later. Check their websites or call ahead to confirm their hours.

Is it safe to walk around NYC early in the morning?

Generally, NYC is a safe city to walk around in. However, it's always a good idea to be aware of your surroundings and take precautions such as walking in well-lit areas and avoiding isolated areas.

Can I see the sunrise in NYC?

Yes, you can see the sunrise in NYC. The exact time varies depending on the season, but generally it occurs between 5:30am and 7am.

Now that you know what to do early morning in NYC, it's time to start planning your next visit. Whether you're looking for exercise, relaxation, or just a cup of coffee, the city has something for everyone. So set your alarm and get ready to start your day in the city that never sleeps.
