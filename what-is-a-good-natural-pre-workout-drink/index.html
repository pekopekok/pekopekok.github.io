What Is A Good Natural Pre Workout Drink

Written by April Mar 09, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking for a natural way to boost your energy before a workout? Look no further! In this article, we’ll explore what a good natural pre workout drink is and how it can benefit your fitness routine. Here’s a table of contents for your convenience:

  • Benefits of a Natural Pre Workout Drink
  • Ingredients to Look For
  • Recipes for Natural Pre Workout Drinks
  • Tips for Making and Drinking Pre Workout Drinks
  • FAQs

Benefits of a Natural Pre Workout Drink

A pre workout drink can help you power through your exercise routine by providing your body with the necessary nutrients and energy. Unlike synthetic pre workout supplements, natural drinks are made with whole food ingredients that are easy to digest and provide a sustainable energy source. Additionally, natural pre workout drinks can help:

  • Improve endurance
  • Enhance focus and mental clarity
  • Increase muscle strength and power
  • Reduce muscle soreness and fatigue

Ingredients to Look For

When choosing a natural pre workout drink, look for ingredients that are rich in carbohydrates, protein, and antioxidants. Here are some examples:

  • Fruits like bananas, berries, and dates
  • Nuts and seeds like almonds, chia seeds, and hemp hearts
  • Leafy greens like spinach and kale
  • Protein powders made from plant-based sources like pea or rice protein
  • Herbs like matcha green tea, ginger, and turmeric

Recipes for Natural Pre Workout Drinks

Berry Banana Smoothie

This smoothie is packed with antioxidants and energy-boosting carbohydrates.

  • 1 cup mixed berries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 scoop plant-based protein powder
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • Ice (optional)

Blend all ingredients in a blender until smooth. Enjoy!

Matcha Green Tea Latte

This latte provides a caffeine boost and the antioxidant benefits of matcha green tea.

  • 1 tsp matcha green tea powder
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1-2 tsp honey or maple syrup

Heat the almond milk in a saucepan over medium heat until hot but not boiling. Whisk in the matcha powder and sweetener until well combined. Serve hot.

Tips for Making and Drinking Pre Workout Drinks

  • Choose ingredients that you enjoy and that agree with your digestion.
  • Drink your pre workout drink 30-60 minutes before your workout for optimal energy.
  • Experiment with different recipes and ingredients to find what works best for you.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water before, during, and after your workout.

FAQs

Can I drink coffee as a pre workout drink?

Yes, coffee can be a good pre workout drink due to its caffeine content. However, be mindful of how much you consume and how it affects your body.

Can I make pre workout drinks in advance?

Yes, pre workout drinks can be made in advance and stored in the fridge for up to 24 hours. Shake or stir before drinking.

Do I need to drink a pre workout drink?

No, a pre workout drink is not necessary for everyone. Some people prefer to exercise on an empty stomach or simply drink water before a workout. Listen to your body and do what feels best for you.

Pros and Cons of Natural Pre Workout Drinks

Pros:

  • Natural ingredients are easier to digest and provide sustainable energy
  • Rich in nutrients and antioxidants that support overall health
  • Customizable recipes to suit your tastes and preferences

Cons:

  • May not provide as much of a caffeine or energy boost as synthetic pre workout supplements
  • Can be time-consuming to prepare
  • May not be suitable for people with certain allergies or dietary restrictions

In conclusion, a good natural pre workout drink can provide a sustainable and nutritious source of energy for your workouts. Experiment with different ingredients and recipes to find what works best for you, and don’t forget to stay hydrated!
