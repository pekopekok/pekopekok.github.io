Best Fitness Apps for Over 60s UK

As we age, staying active and healthy becomes even more important. With the help of technology, there are now plenty of fitness apps available that can help seniors in the UK stay fit and healthy. In this article, we will highlight some of the best fitness apps for over 60s UK.

Table of Content

  • MyFitnessPal
  • Fitbit
  • Yoga Studio
  • MapMyWalk

MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is a free app that allows users to track their nutrition and exercise. It has a database of over 6 million foods and allows users to enter their own recipes. The app also tracks steps and workouts, making it easy for seniors to stay active throughout the day. MyFitnessPal is available on both iOS and Android devices.

How to Use MyFitnessPal

To use MyFitnessPal, simply download the app and create an account. Enter your personal information, such as age, height, and weight, and set your goals. The app will then give you a daily calorie goal and allow you to track your food intake and exercise.

Tips

  • Be honest about your food intake and exercise.
  • Use the app consistently to see results.
  • Connect with friends for support and motivation.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Free to use.
  • Easy to track nutrition and exercise.
  • Large database of foods.

Cons

  • Can be time-consuming to enter food intake.
  • May not be suitable for those with limited tech experience.

Fitbit

Fitbit is a popular fitness app that tracks activity, sleep, and heart rate. It also allows users to track their food intake and set goals. Fitbit is available on both iOS and Android devices and has a variety of devices to choose from, including watches and fitness trackers.

How to Use Fitbit

To use Fitbit, download the app and create an account. Connect your Fitbit device to the app and start tracking your activity, sleep, and heart rate. You can also track your food intake and set goals.

Tips

  • Set achievable goals.
  • Use the app to track your progress.
  • Connect with friends for support and motivation.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Tracks a variety of metrics.
  • Easy to use.
  • Comes with a variety of devices to choose from.

Cons

  • Some devices can be expensive.
  • May not be suitable for those with limited tech experience.

Yoga Studio

Yoga Studio is a yoga app that offers a variety of classes for all levels. It also allows users to create their own custom classes. Yoga Studio is available on both iOS and Android devices.

How to Use Yoga Studio

To use Yoga Studio, download the app and create an account. Browse the variety of classes offered or create your own custom class. Follow along with the instructor and enjoy the benefits of yoga.

Tips

  • Start with beginner classes if you're new to yoga.
  • Listen to your body and don't push yourself too hard.
  • Practice regularly to see the benefits of yoga.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Offers a variety of classes for all levels.
  • Allows users to create their own custom classes.
  • Easy to follow along with the instructor.

Cons

  • May not be suitable for those with limited mobility.
  • Some classes may be too challenging for beginners.

MapMyWalk

MapMyWalk is a fitness app that allows users to track their walks and hikes. It also offers a variety of challenges and allows users to connect with friends for support and motivation. MapMyWalk is available on both iOS and Android devices.

How to Use MapMyWalk

To use MapMyWalk, download the app and create an account. Start tracking your walks and hikes and join challenges to stay motivated. Connect with friends for added support and motivation.

Tips

  • Start with shorter walks if you're new to walking.
  • Set achievable goals.
  • Connect with friends for added support and motivation.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Easy to track walks and hikes.
  • Offers a variety of challenges for added motivation.
  • Connect with friends for added support and motivation.

Cons

  • May not be suitable for those with limited mobility.
  • Some challenges may be too difficult for beginners.

In conclusion, there are plenty of fitness apps available for seniors in the UK. Whether you're tracking your nutrition and exercise with MyFitnessPal, staying active with Fitbit or MapMyWalk, or practicing yoga with Yoga Studio, there's an app out there for everyone. Give these apps a try and see how they can help you stay fit and healthy as you age.
