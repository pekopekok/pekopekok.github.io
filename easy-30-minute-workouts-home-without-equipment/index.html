How to

Before we get into the exercises, it's important to understand how to structure an effective at-home workout. Here are some tips:

  • Set a timer for 30 minutes
  • Choose 4-5 exercises to perform
  • Perform each exercise for 30 seconds, and then rest for 30 seconds
  • Repeat the circuit for 30 minutes

By following this structure, you'll be able to get a full-body workout in just 30 minutes. Now, let's move on to the exercises!

Step by Step

Here are some easy 30 minute workouts home without equipment:

Squats
