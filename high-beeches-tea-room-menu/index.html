How to Order from High Beeches Tea Room Menu

Ordering from our High Beeches Tea Room menu is easy-peasy. Simply head to our website and browse through our selection of tasty treats. Once you've made your choice, click on the 'Add to Cart' button and proceed to checkout. You can also give us a call to place your order over the phone. Our friendly staff will be happy to help you out.

At the High Beeches Tea Room, we offer a range of options to cater to all dietary needs. Whether you're vegan, gluten-free, or have specific allergies, we've got you covered. Just let us know your requirements when placing your order, and we'll make sure to accommodate them.

Step by Step Guide to Ordering Online

Here's a step-by-step guide to ordering online from our High Beeches Tea Room menu:

  1. Visit our website and browse through our menu options.
  2. Select your preferred dish and click on 'Add to Cart'.
  3. Once you've added all items to your cart, click on the 'Checkout' button.
  4. Fill in your details, including name, address, and payment information.
  5. Double-check your order details and then click on 'Place Order'.
  6. Wait for your delicious treats to arrive!

Top High Beeches Tea Room Menu Recommendations

With so many delicious options on our menu, it can be hard to know where to start. Here are a few of our top recommendations:

Beeches Breakfast

Our hearty Beeches Breakfast is the perfect way to start your day. It includes your choice of eggs, bacon, sausage, black pudding, hash browns, mushrooms, and grilled tomato. Served with a slice of toast, this dish is sure to keep you full and satisfied for hours.

Cornish Pasty

Our Cornish Pasty is a classic dish that's loved by all. Made with flaky pastry and filled with tender beef, potato, swede, and onion, this dish is sure to warm you up on a chilly day.

Afternoon Tea

Our Afternoon Tea is the perfect treat for a lazy afternoon. It includes a selection of finger sandwiches, scones with jam and clotted cream, and a variety of sweet treats. Served with your choice of tea or coffee, this is the ultimate indulgence.

FAQs About High Beeches Tea Room Menu

Q: Do you offer vegan options?

A: Yes, we offer a range of vegan options on our menu, including our Vegan Breakfast, Vegan Sausage Roll, and Vegan Afternoon Tea.

Q: Are your ingredients locally sourced?

A: We pride ourselves on using locally sourced ingredients whenever possible. This helps us to support local businesses and ensures that our food is fresh and of the highest quality.

Q: Can I order online for pickup?

A: Yes, you can order online for pickup. Simply select the 'Pickup' option at checkout and choose your preferred pickup time.

Q: Do you offer catering services?

A: Yes, we offer catering services for events of all sizes. Whether you're planning a wedding, corporate event, or birthday party, we can provide delicious food and drinks to make your event extra special. Contact us for more information.
