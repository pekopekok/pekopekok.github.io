What is the ABC Saturday Morning Cartoon Lineup?

The ABC Saturday Morning Cartoon Lineup was a block of animated TV shows that aired on ABC during Saturday mornings. It was a staple for kids growing up in the '80s and '90s, and it featured some of the most iconic cartoons of all time. The lineup ran from 1955 until it was discontinued in 2011, but the golden era was in the '80s and '90s.
