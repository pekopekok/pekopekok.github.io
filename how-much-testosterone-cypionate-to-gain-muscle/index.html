What Is Testosterone Cypionate?

Testosterone cypionate is a synthetic version of the male hormone testosterone. It's commonly used to treat low testosterone levels in men, but it's also used off-label for bodybuilding and athletic performance enhancement. Testosterone cypionate is an injectable steroid, and it's available in various strengths.

How Much Testosterone Cypionate to Gain Muscle?

The amount of testosterone cypionate you need to gain muscle depends on several factors, including your age, weight, and fitness goals. The side effects of testosterone cypionate include acne, fluid retention, gynecomastia, and high blood pressure. It can also increase the risk of heart disease, liver damage, and prostate cancer.
