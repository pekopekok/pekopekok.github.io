What is Five A Day Fitness Disco en Francais?

Five A Day Fitness Disco en Francais is a fitness program that incorporates dance, music, and French language learning. The program is designed for all fitness levels and ages, and no prior knowledge of French is required.

Five A Day Fitness Disco en Francais can be done in the comfort of your own home or in a group setting.
