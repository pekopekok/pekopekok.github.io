Description

Chloe Ting is a fitness influencer with a massive online following. Her ab workout is a 10-minute routine that targets the core muscles. The workout consists of 10 different exercises, each performed for 30 seconds with a 5-second rest in between. The exercises include crunches, leg raises, and plank variations.

How to

To perform Chloe Ting's ab workout, you will need a mat and a timer. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Warm up

Before starting the workout, it is essential to warm up your muscles. You can do this by jogging in place, jumping jacks, or any other cardio exercise that gets your heart rate up.

Step 2: Begin the workout

Set your timer for 10 minutes and start the workout. Perform each exercise for 30 seconds, with a 5-second rest in between. Repeat the circuit twice for a total of 10 minutes.

Step 3: Cool down

After completing the workout, it is crucial to cool down your muscles. You can do this by stretching or doing yoga poses that target the core muscles.

Tips

If you want to maximize your calorie burn during Chloe Ting's ab workout, here are some tips:

  • Engage your core muscles during each exercise.
  • Focus on your form to ensure you are using the correct muscles.
  • Increase the intensity by adding weights or resistance bands.
  • Incorporate cardio exercises into your routine to boost your calorie burn.

Solution

So, how many calories does Chloe Ting's ab workout burn? The answer depends on several factors, such as your body weight, age, and fitness level. However, on average, Chloe Ting's ab workout can burn around 50-100 calories in 10 minutes. This may not seem like a lot, but it can add up over time if you consistently perform the workout.
