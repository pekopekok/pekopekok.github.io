How to Get Good Morning America Tickets

Attending a Good Morning America taping requires tickets, and there are a few ways to get them. Here are your options:

1. Online Request Form

The easiest way to request tickets is to fill out Good Morning America's online request form. You can find it on the show's official website. The form will ask for your name, contact information, and preferred taping date. You'll also need to answer a few questions about why you want to attend and what makes you a good candidate for the audience.

After submitting the form, you'll receive an email confirming your request. If your request is approved, you'll receive another email with your ticket information.

2. Standby Tickets

If you're unable to secure tickets through the online request form, you can try for standby tickets. These tickets are available on a first-come, first-served basis on the day of the taping. To get standby tickets, you'll need to arrive at the studio early in the morning, usually around 6 am. You'll be given a number and will need to wait until the staff begins distributing tickets.

Keep in mind that standby tickets do not guarantee entry into the taping. It's possible that you'll be turned away if the studio is at capacity.

How Much Are Good Morning America Tickets?

The good news is that Good Morning America tickets are free. There is no cost to attend a taping. However, keep in mind that standby tickets do require you to arrive early and wait in line, which can be a time commitment.

Tips for Securing Your Spot in the Audience

Here are a few tips to help you secure your spot in the audience:

1. Request tickets early

The earlier you request tickets, the better. Good Morning America receives a high volume of requests, and tickets can be limited. Requesting early increases your chances of securing a spot.

2. Be specific about your availability

When filling out the online request form, be as specific as possible about your availability. If you're flexible with dates and times, let the staff know. This can increase your chances of being approved for a ticket.

3. Bring a friend

If you're able to secure tickets, consider bringing a friend. Good Morning America allows you to request up to four tickets per request. Having a companion can make the experience more enjoyable.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What time do I need to arrive for standby tickets?

You'll need to arrive at the studio around 6 am for standby tickets. The staff will begin distributing tickets on a first-come, first-served basis.

2. Can I request tickets for a specific date?

Yes, you can request tickets for a specific date using the online request form. However, keep in mind that availability may be limited.

3. How long is the taping?

The taping typically lasts around two hours.

Pros and Cons of Attending a Good Morning America Taping

Pros:

  • Chance to see your favorite celebrities and musicians
  • Opportunity to be on TV

Cons:

  • Time commitment for standby tickets
  • No guarantee of entry with standby tickets
  • Early morning arrival time
