Things To Do On Valentine s Day In New Orleans A Relaxed Guide

Valentine's Day is just around the corner, and if you're planning to spend it in New Orleans, you're in for a treat! The city is full of romantic, fun, and unique activities that you and your loved one can enjoy together. In this guide, we'll give you some ideas on what to do on Valentine's Day in New Orleans. From delicious food to exciting activities, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Food and Drinks
  • Activities
  • Romantic Spots
  • Events

Food and Drinks

New Orleans is known for its incredible cuisine, and there's no better way to celebrate Valentine's Day than by indulging in some delicious food and drinks. Here are some ideas:

1. Brunch at Brennan's

Start your Valentine's Day off right with brunch at Brennan's. This iconic New Orleans restaurant is known for its delicious Creole cuisine and legendary cocktails. Try the Eggs Benedict or the Bananas Foster French Toast, and don't forget to order a Bloody Mary or a Mimosa.

2. Dinner at Galatoire's

If you're looking for a romantic dinner spot, Galatoire's is the perfect choice. This elegant restaurant has been a New Orleans institution since 1905 and is known for its impeccable service and classic Creole cuisine. Be sure to try the Oysters Rockefeller and the Shrimp Remoulade.

3. Drinks at the Carousel Bar

The Carousel Bar & Lounge is located in the historic Hotel Monteleone and is famous for its revolving bar. Grab a seat and enjoy a classic cocktail while you take in the stunning views of the French Quarter.

Activities

Looking for something fun to do on Valentine's Day? New Orleans has plenty of activities to choose from. Here are some ideas:

1. Jazz Cruise on the Mississippi River

Take a romantic jazz cruise on the Mississippi River with the Steamboat Natchez. Enjoy live jazz music, delicious food, and stunning views of the city as you sail down the river.

2. Ghost Tour of the French Quarter

If you're feeling brave, take a ghost tour of the French Quarter. Explore the city's haunted history while you walk through the streets and alleyways of the oldest neighborhood in New Orleans.

3. Cooking Class at the New Orleans School of Cooking

Learn how to cook like a New Orleans local with a cooking class at the New Orleans School of Cooking. You'll learn how to make classic dishes like gumbo, jambalaya, and pralines.

Romantic Spots

New Orleans is a city full of romantic spots. Whether you're looking for a quiet park or a stunning view, there's something for everyone. Here are some ideas:

1. City Park

Take a stroll through City Park, one of the largest urban parks in the country. Enjoy the beautiful oak trees, the lagoons, and the sculptures scattered throughout the park.

2. The Garden District

Take a walking tour of the Garden District and admire the stunning historic homes and gardens. Be sure to stop by Lafayette Cemetery No. 1, one of the oldest cemeteries in New Orleans.

3. Frenchmen Street

Frenchmen Street is known for its live music and vibrant nightlife, but it's also a great spot for a romantic stroll. Take in the colorful street art and the lively atmosphere while you hold hands with your loved one.

Events

New Orleans is known for its festive atmosphere, and Valentine's Day is no exception. Here are some events you can attend:

1. Valentine's Day Concert at Preservation Hall

Celebrate Valentine's Day with live jazz music at Preservation Hall. This intimate venue is the perfect spot for a romantic evening.

2. Valentine's Day Dinner and Dance at the National WWII Museum

Dance the night away at the National WWII Museum's Valentine's Day Dinner and Dance. Enjoy a delicious dinner, live music, and a dance floor surrounded by historic artifacts.

3. Valentine's Day Parade in the French Quarter

Join the fun at the Valentine's Day Parade in the French Quarter. Watch as the colorful floats and marching bands make their way through the streets, throwing beads and candy to the crowds.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are plenty of things to do on Valentine's Day in New Orleans. Whether you're looking for a romantic dinner spot or a fun activity to do with your loved one, this city has something for everyone. So book your trip, pack your bags, and get ready to fall in love with New Orleans!
