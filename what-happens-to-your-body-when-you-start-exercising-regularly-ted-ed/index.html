What Happens To Your Body When You Start Exercising Regularly Ted Ed

Regular exercise has numerous benefits for the human body. However, many people don’t understand the full extent of what happens to their body when they start exercising regularly. In this article, we’ll dive into what happens to your body when you start exercising regularly TED-Ed and how you can make the most of your exercise routine.

Table of Contents

  • Increased Energy
  • Improved Mood
  • Reduced Stress
  • Better Sleep
  • Stronger Muscles
  • Healthier Heart
  • Improved Flexibility

  • Conclusion

Increased Energy

One of the most immediate benefits of regular exercise is increased energy. When you exercise regularly, your body releases endorphins which make you feel more alert and awake. Additionally, regular exercise improves your cardiovascular health which means your heart and lungs are better equipped to deliver oxygen to your body’s cells. When your cells have more oxygen, they can produce more energy which makes you feel more energized overall.

Improved Mood

Another benefit of regular exercise is an improved mood. When you exercise, your body releases endorphins which make you feel happy and content. Additionally, regular exercise can help reduce symptoms of depression and anxiety. This is because exercise helps regulate the production of stress hormones which can contribute to feelings of depression and anxiety.

Reduced Stress

Regular exercise can also help reduce stress levels. When you exercise, your body produces a hormone called cortisol which helps regulate stress. Additionally, regular exercise can help you feel more relaxed overall which can help reduce stress levels. This is because exercise helps release tension in your muscles which can contribute to feelings of stress.

Better Sleep

Another benefit of regular exercise is better sleep. When you exercise, your body releases endorphins which can help you feel more relaxed and ready for sleep. Additionally, regular exercise can help regulate your sleep cycle which means you’ll be able to fall asleep faster and stay asleep longer. This is because exercise helps regulate the production of hormones such as melatonin which are crucial for a good night’s sleep.

Stronger Muscles

Regular exercise can also help you build stronger muscles. When you exercise, your body breaks down muscle tissue which then rebuilds itself stronger than before. Additionally, regular exercise helps increase your muscle mass which means you’ll be able to lift heavier objects and perform physical activities with more ease.

Healthier Heart

Another benefit of regular exercise is a healthier heart. When you exercise, your heart rate increases which means your heart is working harder to pump blood throughout your body. Additionally, regular exercise can help reduce your risk of heart disease by reducing your blood pressure and cholesterol levels.

Improved Flexibility

Regular exercise can also help improve your flexibility. When you exercise, you’re stretching and moving your body which can help improve your range of motion. Additionally, regular exercise can help reduce your risk of injury by increasing your body’s flexibility and mobility.

Boosted Immune System

Another benefit of regular exercise is a boosted immune system. When you exercise, your body produces antibodies which help fight off illnesses and infections. Additionally, regular exercise can help reduce your risk of chronic diseases such as diabetes and cancer by boosting your immune system.

Weight Loss

Finally, regular exercise can help you lose weight. When you exercise, your body burns calories which can help you lose weight over time. Additionally, regular exercise can help increase your muscle mass which means your body will burn more calories even when you’re not exercising.

Conclusion

Overall, regular exercise has numerous benefits for the human body. From increased energy to weight loss, regular exercise can help improve your physical and mental health in countless ways. So, start exercising regularly today and experience the benefits for yourself!
